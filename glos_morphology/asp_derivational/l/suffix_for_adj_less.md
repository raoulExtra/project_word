# -less

## Definition
Transforms adjectives into adjectives meaning "without" or "lacking."

## Examples
- **care** → **careless**, **hope** → **hopeless**, **fear** → **fearless**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Adjective or noun root
- **Output**: Adjective meaning "without"
