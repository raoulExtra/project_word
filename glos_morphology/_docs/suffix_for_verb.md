# Verb Suffixes in English

## Overview
English verb suffixes are derivational morphemes that transform base words into verb forms, expressing various semantic relationships like action, state, or occurrence.

## Classification

### Action/State Suffixes
- **-en** - convert to verb (make, become)
- **-ify** - make or become
- **-ize/-ise** - make or cause to be

### Verbalizer Suffixes
- **-er** - one who performs (agent nouns, also verb-forming in some cases)
- **-or** - one who performs (agent nouns)
- **-ist** - one who practices or specializes
- **-ee** - one who is acted upon

### State Suffixes
- **-ate** - make or become (adjective/verb)
- **-ize** - make (verb form)
