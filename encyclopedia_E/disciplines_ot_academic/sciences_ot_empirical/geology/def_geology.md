# Geology

## Definition
Geology is the science that studies the Earth's physical structure, substance, history, and processes, including the study of rocks and minerals.

## Details
- **Focus**: Earth's materials, structures, and history
- **Key concepts**: Rocks, minerals, fossils, plate tectonics, geological time
- **Branches**: Petrology, mineralogy, paleontology, structural geology
- **Method**: Field observation, laboratory analysis, dating techniques

## Context
Geology provides insights into Earth's 4.5-billion-year history and the processes that shape our planet's surface and interior.
