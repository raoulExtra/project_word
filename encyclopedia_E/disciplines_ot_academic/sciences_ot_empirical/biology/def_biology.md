# Biology

## Definition
Biology is the natural science that studies life and living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, and evolution.

## Details
- **Focus**: Living organisms and life processes
- **Key concepts**: Cells, genetics, evolution, ecology, physiology
- **Branches**: Botany, zoology, microbiology, genetics, ecology
- **Method**: Observation, experimentation, taxonomy

## Context
Biology studies the diversity of life on Earth and the mechanisms that sustain and transform living systems.
