# Formal

## Definition
Formal refers to the application of rigorous, structured methods and systems for knowledge representation and reasoning, characterized by precise definitions, logical structures, and systematic procedures.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Provide unambiguous, mathematically rigorous frameworks for reasoning about concepts and systems
- **Characteristics**:
  - Explicit definitions and axioms
  - Logical inference systems
  - Formal languages and notations
  - Syntactic and semantic precision
- **Scope**: Encompasses formal logic, mathematics, formal methods in computer science, and systematic conceptual modeling

## Context
The formal aspect provides the foundation for precise knowledge representation, enabling automated reasoning, verification, and consistent communication across disciplines that require rigorous treatment of concepts and relationships.
