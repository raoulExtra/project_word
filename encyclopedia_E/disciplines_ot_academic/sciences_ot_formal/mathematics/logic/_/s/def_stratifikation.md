# Stratifikation

## Definition
Stratifikation (Stratification) is the process of arranging or classifying elements into hierarchical or layered structures based on shared characteristics or levels of complexity.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Organize complex systems into manageable layers
- **Key concepts**: Hierarchy, levels, categorization, layering
- **Applications**: 
  - Database normalization
  - Type systems in programming
  - Social stratification
  - Geological layers
- **Properties**: Each layer builds upon or depends on lower layers

## Context
In formal logic and mathematics, stratification prevents paradoxes and ensures consistency by organizing concepts into well-defined hierarchical levels.
