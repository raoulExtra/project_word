# Disciplin

## Definition
A disciplin is a branch of knowledge or academic field that studies a specific aspect of reality, characterized by its own concepts, methods, and body of established knowledge.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Organizes human knowledge into coherent, studyable domains
- **Characteristics**: 
  - Has defined scope and boundaries
  - Uses specialized terminology
  - Employs specific methodologies
  - Produces scholarly literature
- **Hierarchy**: Disciplines can be grouped into broader categories or subdivided into specialized sub-disciplines

## Context
In the academic knowledge structure, disciplines form the primary organizational units that sit between general foundations and specialized applications. They enable systematic study of reality through focused inquiry.
