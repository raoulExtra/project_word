# Technique

## Definition
A technique is a procedure or method of carrying out a task or achieving a desired outcome, typically involving systematic steps or procedures.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Systematic approach to solving problems or accomplishing tasks
- **Characteristics**: 
  - Reproducible procedures
  - Step-by-step processes
  - Skills applied in specific contexts
- **Relation to method**: Techniques are components of broader methods

## Context
In applied sciences, techniques are specialized procedures developed within specific disciplines to achieve reliable, repeatable results in research or practice.
