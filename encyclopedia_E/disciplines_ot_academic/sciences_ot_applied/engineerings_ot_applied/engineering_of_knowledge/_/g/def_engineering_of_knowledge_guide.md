# Engineering of Knowledge Guide

This guide covers the systematic construction and maintenance of knowledge systems.

## Overview

Engineering of knowledge applies systematic methods to design, build, and maintain knowledge representations, ontologies, and information systems.

## Key Concepts

### Ontology
Formal specification of shared conceptualization.

### Attribute
Distinguishing property or characteristic of an entity.

### Domain
Bounded scope of knowledge or application.

## Principles

1. **Explicitness**: All assumptions clearly stated
2. **Reusability**: Maximize reuse through patterns
3. **Interoperability**: Enable integration with other systems
4. **Modularity**: Separate concerns into independent modules
