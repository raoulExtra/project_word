# Symbol

## Definition
A symbol is a mark, sign, or character that represents something else, typically through convention or interpretation.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Represent objects, concepts, or ideas
- **Key aspects**: Representation, convention, interpretation
- **Types**: Linguistic symbols, mathematical symbols, icons
- **Role**: Enable communication and abstract reasoning

## Context
Symbols are fundamental to language, mathematics, and semiotics. They enable humans to represent and communicate complex ideas through conventional signs.
