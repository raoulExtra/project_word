# Process

## Definition
A process is a series of actions, steps, or operations conducted in a systematic way to achieve a particular outcome or objective.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Transform inputs into desired outputs through organized sequences of actions
- **Key aspects**:
  - Sequential steps or stages
  - Input → Transformation → Output
  - Can be human, mechanical, or automated
- **Types**:
  - Natural processes (biological, physical phenomena)
  - Human processes (deliberate activities)
  - Mechanical processes (machine operations)
  - Computational processes (algorithmic executions)
- **Role**: Fundamental to understanding change, causality, and system behavior

## Context
In core concepts, processes are central to systems theory, scientific methodology, and engineering. They represent how entities interact over time to produce changes, transform resources, and achieve objectives. Understanding processes is essential for modeling dynamic behavior and designing interventions.
