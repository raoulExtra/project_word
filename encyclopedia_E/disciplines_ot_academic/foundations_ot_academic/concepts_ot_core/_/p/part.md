# Part

## Definition
A part is an individual component or element within a larger whole, contributing to the structure and function of that whole through its specific characteristics and relationships.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Serve as building blocks that combine to form complex entities
- **Key aspects**:
  - Component identity (distinct characteristics)
  - Relational role (how it connects to other parts)
  - Functional contribution (what it enables)
- **Types**:
  - Physical parts (tangible components)
  - Abstract parts (conceptual elements)
  - Functional parts (roles or responsibilities)
  - Structural parts (organizational elements)
- **Role**: Enables analysis and understanding of wholes through decomposition

## Context
Parts are fundamental to systems thinking, engineering, and ontology. While structure describes how parts are arranged and function describes what parts do, part identifies the individual elements that make up complex entities. Understanding parts is essential for modular design and component-based thinking.
