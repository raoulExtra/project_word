# Aspect

## Definition
An aspect is a particular part, feature, or dimension of a concept, entity, or system, viewed from a specific perspective or considered in relation to particular qualities, properties, or relationships.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Provide focused perspectives on complex entities or concepts
- **Key aspects**:
  - Perspective (specific viewpoint)
  - Dimension (particular feature)
  - Decomposition (breaking down complexity)
  - Context-dependence (relevant to specific purposes)
- **Types**:
  - Theoretical aspect (abstract consideration)
  - Practical aspect (real-world application)
  - Structural aspect (organizational features)
  - Functional aspect (purpose or role)
- **Role**: Enable systematic analysis by allowing examination of entities through different lenses

## Context
Aspects are fundamental to conceptual modeling, ontology engineering, and systematic analysis. They allow complex entities to be understood through focused examination of particular dimensions or features, supporting both reductionist and holistic approaches to understanding.
