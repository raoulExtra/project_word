# Meaning

## Definition
Meaning is the dictionary meaning of a word, phrase, sign, or symbol - the thing or idea that is expressed by language or symbols.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Convey information, express ideas, communicate
- **Key aspects**: Reference, interpretation, context
- **Types**: Lexical meaning, sentence meaning, pragmatic meaning
- **Role**: Enable communication and understanding

## Context
Meaning is central to linguistics, philosophy of language, and semiotics. It bridges the gap between symbols and the concepts they represent.
