# Relation

## Definition
A relation is an association, connection, or correspondence between two or more entities, defining how they interact, correspond, or are otherwise connected in structure or meaning.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Express connections between entities
- **Key aspects**:
  - Binary (between two entities) or n-ary (between multiple entities)
  - Can be symmetric, antisymmetric, or transitive
  - May be functional (one-to-one, one-to-many, many-to-many)
- **Types**:
  - Spatial relations (above, below, between)
  - Temporal relations (before, after, during)
  - Logical relations (implication, equivalence)
  - Semantic relations (synonymy, hyponymy)
- **Properties**: Reflexive, symmetric, transitive, antisymmetric

## Context
Relations are fundamental to mathematics, logic, and linguistics, providing the framework for describing structure, order, and meaning across all domains of knowledge.
