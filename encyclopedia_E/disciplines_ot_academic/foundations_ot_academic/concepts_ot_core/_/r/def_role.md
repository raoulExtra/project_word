# Role

## Definition
A role is a set of expectations, responsibilities, capabilities, or functions that define the position or capacity of an entity within a system, relationship, or context.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Define positions and functions within systems and relationships
- **Key aspects**:
  - Assigned to entities based on their capabilities or context
  - May be static or dynamic within a system
  - Can be explicit (formally defined) or implicit (emergent)
  - May change over time or across contexts
- **Types**:
  - System roles (controller, observer, actor)
  - Relational roles (parent, child, teacher, student)
  - Functional roles (input provider, output consumer)
  - Structural roles (node, hub, peripheral)
- **Properties**: Context-dependent, may have overlapping boundaries, can be shared or exclusive

## Context
Roles are fundamental to systems thinking, organizational theory, and social sciences, providing a framework for understanding how entities participate in and contribute to larger structures and relationships.
