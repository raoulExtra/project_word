# Core Concepts Guide

This guide covers fundamental concepts that serve as building blocks for knowledge representation and ontology engineering.

## Overview

Core concepts are the most basic elements of thought and knowledge organization. They provide the foundation for more specialized domains and applications.

## Key Concepts

### Concept
Mental constructs that represent categories, properties, or relationships.

### Entity
Distinct, identifiable objects or beings that exist as unified wholes.

### Relation
Connections or associations between entities or concepts.

### Property
Attributes or characteristics of entities or concepts.

## Aspects

### Conceptual
Mental representations and knowledge organization.

### Complexity
Degree of interconnection and emergent behavior in systems.

## Principles

1. **Abstraction**: Simplify complex reality to essential features
2. **Categorization**: Group similar items for analysis
3. **Reference**: Establish connections between symbols and reality
