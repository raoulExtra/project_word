# Role

## Definition
A role is a function, position, or responsibility that defines the behavior, purpose, or contribution of an entity within a model, system, or conceptual framework, specifying what the entity does and how it interacts with other components.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Define specific functions and responsibilities within models and systems
- **Key aspects**:
  - Behavioral specification (what the entity does)
  - Positional definition (where the entity fits)
  - Interaction patterns (how the entity relates to others)
- **Types**:
  - Structural roles (defining position in architecture)
  - Functional roles (defining purpose and behavior)
  - Relational roles (defining relationships with other entities)

## Context
Roles are essential for clarifying responsibilities and behaviors in conceptual models, enabling clearer understanding of how systems operate and how components interact.
