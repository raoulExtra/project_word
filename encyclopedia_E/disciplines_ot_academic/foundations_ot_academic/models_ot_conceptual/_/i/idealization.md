# Idealization

## Definition
Idealization is the process of creating simplified or perfect versions of reality by removing real-world complications, imperfections, or constraints to focus on essential principles.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Enable analysis by removing complicating factors
- **Key aspects**:
  - Simplification (removing complexity)
  - Perfection (assuming ideal conditions)
  - Abstraction (focusing on essentials)
- **Types**:
  - Physical idealization (perfect materials)
  - Mathematical idealization (idealized relationships)
  - Behavioral idealization (optimal strategies)
- **Role**: Tool for understanding fundamental principles without distraction

## Context
Idealization is a powerful modeling technique, but must be used carefully. Results from idealized models need validation against real-world conditions.

**Related terms**: model, abstraction, assumption
