# Time

## Definition
Time is a fundamental continuum that allows events to be ordered from past through present to future, enabling measurement, duration, and the sequencing of change.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Sequence events, measure durations, enable causality
- **Key aspects**:
  - Dimension of existence
  - Irreversible flow (arrow of time)
  - Measurable units (seconds, minutes, hours)
- **Types**:
  - Physical time (in physics)
  - Social time (cultural constructions)
  - Psychological time (perception-based)
- **Properties**: Linear vs cyclical, absolute vs relative

## Context
Time is central to all disciplines - physics (spacetime), biology (evolution), psychology (memory/cognition), and social sciences (historiography).
