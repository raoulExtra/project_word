# Property

## Definition
A property is an attribute, characteristic, or quality of an object, system, or phenomenon that can be measured, observed, or described.

## Details
- **Characteristics**:
  - Describes attributes of entities
  - Can be qualitative or quantitative
  - Observable or measurable
  - Intrinsic or extrinsic
- **Types**:
  - Physical properties (mass, color, density)
  - Chemical properties (reactivity, melting point)
  - Mathematical properties (even, prime, continuous)
- **Purpose**: Provides distinguishing characteristics for identification and classification

## Context
In academic and scientific contexts, properties are fundamental descriptors used to characterize, classify, and understand the nature of objects, substances, and abstract concepts.
