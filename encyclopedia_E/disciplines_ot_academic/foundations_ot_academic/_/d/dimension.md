# Dimension

## Definition
A dimension is a fundamental parameter or measurable extent that defines the nature and structure of an object, space, or system, providing the framework for measurement and description.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Define spatial or conceptual extent
- **Key aspects**:
  - Spatial dimensions (length, width, height)
  - Time as the fourth dimension
  - Abstract dimensions (degrees of freedom, parameter spaces)
- **Types**:
  - Cartesian dimensions (x, y, z coordinates)
  - Dimensional analysis (fundamental quantities)
  - Phase space dimensions (state variables)
- **Role**: Foundation for modeling and understanding complex systems

## Context
Dimensions provide the structural framework for describing and analyzing systems across mathematics, physics, and conceptual modeling.
