# Statement

## Definition
A statement is a declarative sentence that is either true or false, serving as a building block for logical reasoning and mathematical proofs.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Express propositions in formal systems
- **Characteristics**:
  - Declarative (not interrogative or imperative)
  - Truth-valued (true or false)
  - Can be simple or compound
- **Types**:
  - Atomic statements (simple propositions)
  - Compound statements (connected by logical operators)
- **Role**: Foundation for logical deduction and proof construction

## Context
In formal sciences, statements are the basic units of logical discourse, enabling the construction of arguments, proofs, and mathematical theories.
