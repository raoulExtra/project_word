# Space

## Definition
Space is the three-dimensional extent in which all objects and events occur and move, providing the framework for physical location, distance, and geometric relationships.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Provide context for physical and abstract positioning
- **Key aspects**:
  - Three dimensions (length, width, height)
  - Infinite extent (in physics)
  - Relative positioning (distance, direction)
- **Types**:
  - Physical space (3D Euclidean)
  - Spacetime (4D with time)
  - Abstract space (mathematical/vector spaces)
  - Social space (conceptual/geographical)
- **Properties**: Homogeneous, isotropic, continuous

## Context
Space is fundamental to physics, mathematics, geography, and social sciences, providing the framework for understanding position, motion, and structure.
