# -ly

## Definition
Transforms adjectives into adverbs meaning "in a manner or way specified by."

## Examples
- **quick** → **quickly**, **happy** → **happily**, **true** → **truly**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Adjective root
- **Output**: Adverb expressing manner, place, time, or degree
