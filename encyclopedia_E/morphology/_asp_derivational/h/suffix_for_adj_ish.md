# -ish

## Definition
Transforms adjectives into adjectives meaning "like," "somewhat," or "approximately."

## Examples
- **child** → **childish**, **red** → **reddish**, **tall** → **tallish**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Noun or adjective root
- **Output**: Adjective meaning "like" or "somewhat"
