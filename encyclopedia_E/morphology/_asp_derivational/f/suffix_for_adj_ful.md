# -ful

## Definition
Transforms adjectives into adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."

## Examples
- **joy** → **joyful**, **fear** → **fearful**, **hope** → **hopeful**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Noun or adjective root
- **Output**: Adjective meaning "full of" or "characterized by"
