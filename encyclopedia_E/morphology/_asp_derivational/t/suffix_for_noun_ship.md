# -ship

## Definition
The suffix **-ship** is a derivational suffix that transforms adjectives into nouns, meaning "state or condition of," "level of," or "group of."

## Details
- **Part of speech**: Suffix (derivational morpheme)
- **Function**: State noun creator - creates nouns expressing state or condition
- **Semantic effect**: Indicates state, level, or membership
- **Examples**:
  - **lead** → **leadership** (state of leading)
  - **citizen** → **citizenship** (state of being citizen)
  - **teacher** → **teachership** (state of teaching)

## Pattern
- **Input**: Adjective or noun root
- **Output**: Noun expressing state, condition, or membership

## Context
In morphology and word formation studies, -ship represents a suffix that creates nouns expressing social roles, states, or conditions.
