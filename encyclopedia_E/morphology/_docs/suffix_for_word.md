# Word-Level Suffixes in English

## Overview
Word-level suffixes are derivational morphemes that transform base words (typically roots, stems, or other words) into new word forms, including inflectional changes and derivational patterns.

## Classification

### Inflectional Suffixes (Word-Level Changes)
- **-s** - plural nouns, 3rd person verbs
- **-ed** - past tense, past participle
- **-ing** - present participle, gerund
- **-er** - comparative adjectives, agent nouns
- **-est** - superlative adjectives
- **-ly** - adverbs (from adjectives)

### Derivational Suffixes (Category Changes)
- **-ful** - adjective from noun
- **-less** - adjective from adjective
- **-able/-ible** - adjective from verb
- **-al** - adjective/verb from noun
- **-ic** - adjective from noun
- **-ous** - adjective from noun
- **-ish** - adjective from adjective/noun
- **-y** - noun from adjective
