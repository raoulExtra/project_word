# -est (Superlative)

## Definition
The suffix **-est** is an inflectional suffix that forms the superlative form of adjectives and adverbs.

## Details
- **Function**: Superlative adjective/adverb marker
- **Examples**:
  - **big** → **biggest** (superlative adjective)
  - **fast** → **fastest** (superlative adverb)

## Pattern
- **Standard**: Add -est to adjective (big → biggest)
- **Irregular**: good → best, bad → worst
- **Consonant doubling**: single syllable with stressed vowel (hot → hottest)
