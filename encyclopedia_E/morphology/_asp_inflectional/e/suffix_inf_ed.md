# -ed (Past Tense)

## Definition
The suffix **-ed** is an inflectional suffix that forms the past tense and past participle of regular verbs.

## Details
- **Function**: Past tense marker
- **Examples**:
  - **walk** → **walked** (past tense)
  - **play** → **played** (past participle)

## Pattern
- **Regular verbs**: Add -ed to base form (walk → walked)
- **CVC pattern**: Double final consonant (run → ran, but stop → stopped)
- **Exceptions**: Irregular verbs have unique forms (go → went, eat → ate)
