# -er (Comparative)

## Definition
The suffix **-er** is an inflectional suffix that forms the comparative form of adjectives and adverbs.

## Details
- **Function**: Comparative adjective/adverb marker
- **Examples**:
  - **big** → **bigger** (comparative adjective)
  - **fast** → **faster** (comparative adverb)

## Pattern
- **Standard**: Add -er to adjective (big → bigger)
- **Irregular**: good → better, bad → worse
- **Consonant doubling**: single syllable with stressed vowel (hot → hotter)
