# -ly (Adverb)

## Definition
The suffix **-ly** is an inflectional suffix that forms adverbs from adjectives, typically meaning "in a manner or way specified by."

## Details
- **Function**: Adverbializer - creates adverbs from adjectives
- **Examples**:
  - **quick** → **quickly** (in a quick manner)
  - **happy** → **happily** (in a happy manner)

## Pattern
- **Standard**: Add -ly to adjective (quick → quickly)
- **Irregular**: good → well, fast → fast
- **Doubling**: stress on last syllable (pretty → prettily)
