# -s (Plural/3rd Person)

## Definition
The suffix **-s** is an inflectional suffix that forms plurals of nouns and 3rd person singular present tense of verbs.

## Details
- **Function**: Plural marker for nouns, 3rd person marker for verbs
- **Examples**:
  - **cat** → **cats** (plural noun)
  - **dog** → **dogs** (plural noun)
  - **run** → **runs** (3rd person verb)

## Pattern
- **Nouns**: Add -s to singular (cat → cats)
- **Verbs**: Add -s to 3rd person singular (run → runs)
- **Exceptions**: Words ending in -s, -x, -z, -ch, -sh often add -es
