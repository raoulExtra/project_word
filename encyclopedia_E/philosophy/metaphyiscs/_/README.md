# Metaphysics Core Glossary

Concepts and aspects fundamental to metaphysical inquiry.

## Concepts

- [Metaphysics](m/metaphysics.md) - The branch investigating fundamental nature of reality
- [Reality](r/reality.md) - The state of things as they actually exist
- [World](w/world.md) - A complete, coherent structure or reality
- [Whole](w/whole.md) - A complete entity containing parts
- [Part](p/part.md) - A portion or component of a whole
- [Identity](i/identity.md) - Numerical sameness over time and change
- [Existence](e/existence.md) - The state of being real or actual

## Aspects

- [Actual](a/asp_actual.md) - That which exists or occurs in fact
- [Possible](p/asp_possible.md) - That which is contingently realizable
- [Existential](e/asp_existential.md) - Pertaining to existence and being
- [Transcendental](t/asp_transcendental.md) - Abstract reasoning, conceptual foundations
- [Metaphysical](m/asp_metaphysical.md) - Fundamental nature of reality
