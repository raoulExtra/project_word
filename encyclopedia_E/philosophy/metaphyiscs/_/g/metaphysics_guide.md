# Metaphysics Guide

The branch of philosophy investigating the fundamental nature of reality.

## Overview

Metaphysics addresses questions that lie at the foundation of all scientific and ordinary knowledge: What exists? What is the nature of reality? How do we know?

## Core Areas

- **Ontology**: What exists and how entities can be grouped
- **Modality**: Possibility, necessity, and contingency
- **Identity and Change**: Persistence through time
- **Causation**: Relationships between events and states
- **Space and Time**: Fundamental structure of the world
- **Mind and Matter**: Relationship between mental and physical

## Key Distinctions

- Actual vs Possible
- Concrete vs Abstract
- Necessary vs Contingent
