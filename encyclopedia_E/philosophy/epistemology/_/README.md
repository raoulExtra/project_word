# Epistemology Glossary

Concepts and aspects in the study of knowledge.

## Concepts

- [Knowledge](k/knowledge.md) - Justified true belief about the world
- [Evidence](e/evidence.md) - Facts supporting knowledge claims
- [Justification](j/justification.md) - Rational grounds for accepting beliefs
- [Belief](b/belief.md) - Mental acceptance of a proposition
- [Truth](t/truth_e.md) - Accurate representation of reality
- [Perception](p/perception.md) - Process of interpreting sensory information
