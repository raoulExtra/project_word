# Epistemology Guide

The study of knowledge: its nature, sources, limits, and validity.

## Overview

Epistemology addresses fundamental questions about knowledge: What is knowledge? How do we acquire it? Can we be certain?

## Core Questions

- What is knowledge (vs. belief, opinion, information)?
- What are the sources of knowledge (perception, reason, testimony)?
- What are the limits of knowledge (skepticism, uncertainty)?
- How is knowledge justified and validated?

## Key Concepts

- **Justification**: The process of supporting beliefs
- **Evidence**: Facts used to support knowledge claims
- **Truth**: The goal of knowledge
- **Skepticism**: Doubt about the possibility of knowledge
