# Epistemology

The branch of philosophy concerned with the nature, sources, and limits of knowledge.

## Overview

Epistemology investigates what knowledge is, how it is acquired, and whether it is possible. It addresses fundamental questions about the justification and validity of belief.

## Key Areas

- **Analysis of Knowledge**: Defining knowledge as justified true belief
- **Sources of Knowledge**: Perception, memory, reason, testimony
- **Social Epistemology**: Knowledge in community and collective contexts
- **Skepticism**: Doubt about the possibility of knowledge
