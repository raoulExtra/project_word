## Rationale

Effective reasoning relies on precise concept application. Encyclopedia E supports this by enabling OO-like enrichment of concepts through explicit, formally defined aspects. We apply concepts more effectively by combining them with dynamically activated aspects that tailor each concept's focus to the current thinking process. These aspects anchor interpretation and ensure consistent application.

## Expected Benefits
[[AI_LOCK+]]
- **Stronger concept application**: Combining concepts with dynamically activated aspects enables more precise and consistent interpretation across tasks.
- **Higher interpretability**: Explicit aspects make reasoning steps transparent and inspectable for both humans and systems.
- **Reduced ambiguity**: Well‑defined aspects eliminate vague or conflicting interpretations of concepts.
- **Better task alignment**: The right aspects can be selected to match the current thinking process or use case.
- **Improved explainability**: Aspect‑anchored definitions allow justification of decisions with structured conceptual references.
- **Faster ontology evolution**: Explicit aspect definitions make it easier to extend, refine, and maintain the knowledge base.
- **Cross‑domain consistency**: Aspects ensure that concepts behave predictably across different domains and use cases.
