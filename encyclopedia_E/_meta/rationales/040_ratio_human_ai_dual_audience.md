## Rationale

Encyclopedia E serves both human readers and AI systems. While human-readable presentation is essential, we also tighten the ontology elements embedded within the encyclopedia to ensure precise, machine-processable knowledge structures.

## Expected Benefits

- **Dual Usability**: Content optimized for both human comprehension and AI processing
- **Ontology Integrity**: Explicit concept definitions and relationships within articles
- **Machine Readability**: Structured data that AI can directly interpret and apply
- **Consistency**: Unified representation across human-facing and machine-facing layers

## Implementation Approach

### Human-Facing Layer
Maintain clear, accessible language and intuitive organization for human readers.

### Ontology Layer
Embed formal concept definitions, relationships, and metadata that AI systems can directly consume.

### Integration
Ensure tight coupling between human-readable content and underlying ontology elements.
