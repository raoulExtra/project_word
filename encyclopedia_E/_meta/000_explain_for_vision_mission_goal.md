# Vision, Mission, Goal

## Vision

To create a unified, comprehensive encyclopedia that serves as the definitive resource for understanding concepts, their relationships, and their applications across all domains of knowledge.

## Mission

Encyclopedia E exists to:

- **Organize knowledge systematically** through structured, hierarchical concept development
- **Eliminate fragmentation** by establishing canonical articles for each distinct concept
- **Enable cross-domain understanding** through explicit linking and relationship mapping
- **Support AI-assisted curation** with formalized rules and rationales for consistency
- **Ground knowledge** in established sources while enabling novel synthesis

## Goal

Build a living, evolving knowledge base that progresses through structured phases:

1. **Phase 1 - Abstract Concepts**: Establish foundational concepts (Information, Knowledge, Concept, Idea, Meaning, Pattern, Signal, Symbol)
2. **Phase 2 - Ground Elements**: Develop combinations and relationships from Phase 1
3. **Phase 3 - Interpretive Capabilities**: Validate understanding through hierarchical capability layers

## Principles

- **Progressive Development**: Each phase builds upon previous phases without forward dependencies
- **Conceptual Integrity**: Each concept has exactly one canonical representation
- **Cross-Domain Linking**: Concepts spanning domains use explicit linking mechanisms
- **Evidence-Based**: All knowledge claims are supported by evidence and justification

## Success Metrics

- Comprehensive coverage of foundational concepts
- Clear relationship mapping between concepts
- Consistent application of structural rules
- AI-assisted scalability and maintenance
