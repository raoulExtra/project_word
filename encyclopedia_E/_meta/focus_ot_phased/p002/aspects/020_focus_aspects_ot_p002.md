# Project Management Aspects

- **asp_temporal** - time-based planning and scheduling
- **asp_budgetary** - resource allocation and cost management
- **asp_quality** - standards and validation criteria
- **asp_risk** - uncertainty and mitigation strategies
- **asp_stakeholder** - contributor and user engagement
- **asp_scope** - boundaries and deliverables definition
- **asp_processual** - step-by-step execution patterns
