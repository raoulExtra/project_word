# Meta Directory

This directory contains the foundational principles and rules governing Encyclopedia E.

## Structure

- **rationales/** - Documents explaining the "why" behind Encyclopedia E's design decisions
- **rules_for_articles/** - Guidelines and constraints for article content, creation and maintenance

## Purpose

The meta layer defines:
- Why articles should be unified through AI-supported normalization
- Why knowledge should be grounded in Wikimedia information
- Rules that ensure consistency and quality across all entries
- **Prerequisite**: Articles for high-level concepts must exist first, as they have significant influence on AI guiding
