## Rationale

We publish our first version of "encyclopedia E" as a repository to accelerate the practical application of this concept ontology and to immediately enhance AI models' ability to interpret, apply, and explain concepts with greater precision and transparency compared to Wikipedia or trained information alone.

## Expected Benefits

- **Precision**: Improved concept interpretation accuracy
- **Transparency**: Clear reasoning paths for AI decisions
- **Application**: Immediate practical use of the ontology
- **Explainability**: Better articulation of concept relationships

## Implementation Approach

### Normalization
Bringing article entries to a consistent structure/schema — same fields, same format, same depth.

### Consolidation
Merging article entries that cover the same concept from different angles into one canonical entry.

### Alignment
Reconciling article entries across domains so shared terms (e.g. "rule" in law vs cybernetics) are explicitly linked rather than siloed.
