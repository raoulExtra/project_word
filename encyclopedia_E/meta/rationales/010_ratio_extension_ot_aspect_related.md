## Rationale

Effective AI reasoning relies on precise concept application. Encyclopedia E supports this by offering explicit, formally defined aspects alongside of concepts that anchor interpretation and ensure consistent application across tasks.

## Expected Benefits

- **Stronger concept application**: AI systems gain clearer, more consistent ways to interpret and use concepts across tasks.
- **Higher interpretability**: Explicit aspects make the reasoning steps of AI models more transparent and inspectable.
- **Reduced ambiguity**: Well‑defined aspects eliminate vague or conflicting interpretations of concepts.
- **Better task alignment**: Models can select the most relevant conceptual structures for the situation at hand.
- **Improved explainability**: Aspect‑anchored definitions allow AI to justify decisions with structured conceptual references.
- **Faster ontology evolution**: Explicit aspect definitions make it easier to extend, refine, and maintain the knowledge base.
- **Cross‑domain consistency**: Aspects ensure that concepts behave predictably across different domains and use cases.
