# Number Theory

## Definition
Number theory is the study of integers and their properties, including primes, divisibility, and solutions to equations.

## Details
- **Focus**: Natural numbers (N), integers (Z), rational numbers (Q)
- **Key concepts**: Prime numbers, congruences, Diophantine equations
- **Historical**: Developed from ancient Babylon and China, formalized by Greeks
- **Modern**: Attributed to Fermat, Euler, Gauss

## Context
Number theory evolved from numerical calculations and is now a distinct mathematical discipline studying abstract properties of numbers.
