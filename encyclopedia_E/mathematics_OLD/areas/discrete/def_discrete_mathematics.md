# Discrete Mathematics

## Definition
Discrete mathematics is the study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete, rather than continuous.

## Details
- **Focus**: Countable sets, graph theory, combinatorics, logic
- **Key concepts**: Integers, graphs, formal languages, automata
- **Applications**: Computer science, information theory, coding theory
- **Relation**: Bridges pure math and applied computing

## Context
Discrete mathematics provides the theoretical foundation for computer algorithms, data structures, and computational complexity.
