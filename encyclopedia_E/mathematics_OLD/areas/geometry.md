# Geometry

## Definition
Geometry is the study of shapes, sizes, properties of space, and the relationships between different forms.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Study of points, lines, angles, surfaces, and solids
- **Key concepts**: Euclidean geometry, topology, differential geometry, non-Euclidean geometries
- **Historical**: Originated in Ancient Greece, Euclid's Elements foundational
- **Modern**: Includes analytic geometry, algebraic geometry, fractal geometry

## Context
Geometry has been studied since ancient times and remains essential for understanding spatial relationships in mathematics, physics, and engineering.
