# Statistics

## Definition
Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, interpreting, presenting, and organizing data.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Extract insights from data, make informed decisions
- **Key concepts**: Descriptive statistics, inferential statistics, hypothesis testing
- **Branches**: Design of experiments, regression analysis, multivariate analysis
- **Applications**: Scientific research, business, government, healthcare

## Context
Statistics provides tools for understanding data patterns and making evidence-based conclusions from uncertain information.
