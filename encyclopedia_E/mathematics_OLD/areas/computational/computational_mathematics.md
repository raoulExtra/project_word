# Computational Mathematics

## Definition
Computational mathematics studies the representation of mathematical problems in terms of computational procedures and the development of algorithms for their solution.

## Details
- **Focus**: Numerical methods, scientific computing, algorithmic mathematics
- **Key concepts**: Error analysis, complexity theory, parallel computing
- **Applications**: Engineering simulation, data analysis, optimization
- **Relation**: Bridges pure mathematics and practical computation

## Context
Computational mathematics enables the numerical solution of problems too complex for analytical methods, supporting scientific and engineering applications.
