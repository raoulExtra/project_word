# Calculus

## Definition
Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change, encompassing differential and integral calculus.

## Details
- **Differential calculus**: Studies rates of change and slopes of curves
- **Integral calculus**: Studies accumulation of quantities and areas under curves
- **Fundamental theorem**: Links differentiation and integration
- **Development**: 17th century by Newton and Leibniz

## Context
Calculus is essential for modeling continuous systems in physics, engineering, economics, and other quantitative disciplines.
