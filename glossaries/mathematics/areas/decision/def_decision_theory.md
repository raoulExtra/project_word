# Decision Theory

## Definition
Decision theory is the study of an individual's or group's choices, encompassing both the process of making decisions and the analysis of decision-making under uncertainty.

## Details
- **Components**: Normative (prescriptive) and descriptive decision theory
- **Key concepts**: Utility theory, game theory, optimization, risk analysis
- **Decision types**: Sequential, simultaneous, stochastic, multi-criteria
- **Applications**: Economics, operations research, artificial intelligence, psychology

## Context
Decision theory provides mathematical frameworks for optimal choice under uncertainty, supporting rational decision-making in complex environments.
