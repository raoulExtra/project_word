# Mathematics

## Overview
Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, structures, space, and change through logical reasoning and proof.

## Major Areas
- **Number Theory** - integers and their properties
- **Algebra** - symbols and structures
- **Geometry** - shapes and spatial properties
- **Calculus/Analysis** - continuous change and limits
- **Discrete Mathematics** - countable structures
- **Mathematical Logic** - formal reasoning
- **Computational Mathematics** - numerical algorithms
- **Probability** - uncertainty and randomness
- **Statistics** - data analysis
- **Decision Theory** - optimal choice under uncertainty
