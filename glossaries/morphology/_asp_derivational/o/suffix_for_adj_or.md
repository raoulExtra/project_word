# -or

## Definition
Transforms verbs into nouns meaning "one who performs" or "thing that performs."

## Examples
- **teach** → **teacher**, **act** → **actor**, **write** → **writer**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Verb root (typically Latinate)
- **Output**: Agent noun identifying performer
