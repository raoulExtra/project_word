# -ize / -ise

## Definition
Transforms adjectives, nouns, or other bases into verbs meaning "to make," "to cause to be," or "to turn into."

## Examples
- **simple** → **simplify**, **civil** → **civilize**, **modern** → **modernize**

## Pattern
- **Input**: Adjective, noun, or base
- **Output**: Verb expressing action or state change
- **Variation**: -ize (American) vs -ise (British)
