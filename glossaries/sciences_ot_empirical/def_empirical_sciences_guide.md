# Empirical Sciences

## Overview
Empirical sciences study the natural world through observation, experimentation, and measurement.

## Major Branches
- **Physics** - matter, energy, forces, motion
- **Chemistry** - substances, reactions, structure
- **Biology** - living organisms, life processes
- **Geology** - Earth's structure, history, processes
