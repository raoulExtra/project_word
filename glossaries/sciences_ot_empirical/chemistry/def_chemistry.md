# Chemistry

## Definition
Chemistry is the scientific study of the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of substances.

## Details
- **Focus**: Matter and its transformations
- **Key concepts**: Atoms, molecules, bonds, reactions, states of matter
- **Branches**: Organic, inorganic, physical, analytical, biochemistry
- **Method**: Laboratory experiments, spectroscopy, modeling

## Context
Chemistry bridges physics and biology, studying how substances interact and transform through chemical reactions.
