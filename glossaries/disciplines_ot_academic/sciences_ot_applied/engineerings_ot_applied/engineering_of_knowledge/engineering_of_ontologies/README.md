# Engineering of Ontologies Glossary

Concepts related to the systematic construction and maintenance of ontologies.

## Terms

### o/ - Ontology
- [Ontology](o/def_ontology.md) - Formal, explicit specification of a shared conceptualization

### s/ - Structural
- [Structural](s/asp_structural.md) - Arrangement, organization, and configuration of components

### a/ - Aspect
- [Aspect](a/010_def_aspect.md) - Grammatical category expressing temporal structure of situations or events

### d/ - Domain
- [Domain](d/010_def_domain.md) - Distinct area of knowledge, application, or subject matter

### c/ - Context
- [Context](c/010_def_context.md) - Environmental factors determining applicability of assertions

### p/ - Pattern
- [Pattern](p/010_def_pattern.md) - Reusable template for modeling domain concepts and relationships
