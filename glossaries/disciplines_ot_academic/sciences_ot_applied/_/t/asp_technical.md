# Technical

## Definition
Technical refers to the application of systematic knowledge, skills, and methods to create, design, or solve problems in specific domains.

## Details
- **Scope**: Applied knowledge in specialized fields
- **Characteristics**:
  - Systematic and methodical approaches
  - Domain-specific procedures
  - Reproducible practices
- **Purpose**: Bridge between theory and practical application

## Context
In applied sciences, technical knowledge encompasses the specialized methods, tools, and procedures used to implement solutions in specific disciplines.
