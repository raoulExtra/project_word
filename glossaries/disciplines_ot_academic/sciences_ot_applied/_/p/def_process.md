# Process

## Definition
A process is a series of actions, steps, or operations conducted in a systematic way to achieve a particular outcome or objective.

## Details
- **Characteristics**:
  - Sequential steps or stages
  - Input → Transformation → Output
  - Can be human, mechanical, or automated
- **Purpose**: Transform inputs into desired outputs
- **Properties**: Repeatable, measurable, improvable

## Context
In applied sciences, processes are systematic methods for transforming inputs into outputs through organized sequences of actions, forming the foundation for techniques and engineering approaches in research and practical applications.
