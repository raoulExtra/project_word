# Organizational

## Definition
The structure and procedures of an organization, including the arrangement of components and their relationships.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Provide structure for coordinated action
- **Key aspects**: Hierarchy, roles, responsibilities, communication channels
- **Types**: Formal organization, informal organization, matrix organization
- **Role**: Enable efficient coordination and goal achievement

## Context
Organizational aspects are crucial for understanding how systems and groups coordinate to achieve objectives.
