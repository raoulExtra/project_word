# Academic

## Definition
Academic refers to activities, knowledge, and institutions related to formal education, scholarly research, and systematic study within established disciplines and institutions.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Systematically investigate and organize knowledge through scholarly methods
- **Characteristics**:
  - Peer-reviewed processes
  - Institutional frameworks (universities, research centers)
  - Formal methodology and evidence standards
  - Knowledge dissemination through publications
- **Scope**: Encompasses both research (discovery of new knowledge) and teaching (transmission of established knowledge)

## Context
The academic aspect provides the framework within which disciplines operate, defining the standards, practices, and institutional structures that govern scholarly inquiry and knowledge creation.
