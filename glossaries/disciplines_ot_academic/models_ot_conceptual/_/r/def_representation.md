# Representation

## Definition
Representation is the act or process of creating a symbolic, visual, or mathematical form that stands for or corresponds to an object, system, or concept, enabling it to be understood, communicated, or manipulated.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Enable understanding and manipulation of complex phenomena
- **Key aspects**:
  - Symbolic mapping (symbols to meaning)
  - Structural correspondence (form to form)
  - Semantic connection (meaning to meaning)
- **Types**:
  - Mathematical representation (equations, formulas)
  - Visual representation (diagrams, graphs)
  - Linguistic representation (words, descriptions)
  - Computational representation (code, data structures)

## Context
Representation is fundamental to modeling, communication, and computation, bridging the gap between reality and human understanding through various symbolic systems.
