# Idealization

## Definition
Idealization is the process of simplifying a complex reality by removing or modifying details to create a tractable, theoretical representation that focuses on essential features while ignoring complicating factors.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Make complex systems manageable for analysis and understanding
- **Key aspects**:
  - Simplification of assumptions
  - Removal of limiting factors
  - Creation of tractable models
- **Types**:
  - Physical idealizations (frictionless surfaces)
  - Mathematical idealizations (point masses)
  - Conceptual idealizations (perfect rationality)

## Context
Idealization is fundamental to scientific modeling, enabling analysis of core principles without being overwhelmed by real-world complexity.
