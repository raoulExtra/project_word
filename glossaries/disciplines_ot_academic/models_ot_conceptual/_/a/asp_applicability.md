# Applicability

## Definition
Applicability is the quality or state of being applicable, relevant, or suitable to a particular situation, context, or set of conditions within the scope of a model or theoretical framework.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Determine the range of situations where a model is valid and useful
- **Key aspects**:
  - Scope boundaries (what situations it covers)
  - Limitation identification (where it breaks down)
  - Context sensitivity (different in different settings)
- **Assessment criteria**:
  - Domain of definition
  - Range of parameters
  - Assumptions validity

## Context
Understanding applicability is crucial for proper model use, ensuring that models are applied only within their valid scope and limitations are acknowledged.
