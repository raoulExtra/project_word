# Scenario

## Definition
A scenario is a structured narrative or description of a possible future situation, past event, or hypothetical condition, used to explore how a model or system might behave under specific conditions.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Test model behavior under varied conditions and explore possibilities
- **Key aspects**:
  - Context (environmental conditions)
  - Actors (participants or agents)
  - Timeline (temporal structure)
  - Outcomes (expected results)
- **Types**:
  - Future scenarios (predictions)
  - Historical scenarios (reconstructions)
  - Counterfactual scenarios (what-if analysis)
  - Stress scenarios (extreme conditions)
- **Role**: Tool for exploring model implications and testing robustness

## Context
Scenarios are essential for model validation and decision-making. They enable exploration of multiple possibilities without committing to specific assumptions about the future.

**Related terms**: model, hypothesis, experiment
