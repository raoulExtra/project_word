# Representation

## Definition
Representation is the act or process of forming or creating a description, model, or depiction of reality, a system, or an idea in a form suitable for analysis, communication, or computation.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Make abstract or complex phenomena accessible for analysis and communication
- **Key aspects**:
  - Symbolic (using signs and symbols)
  - Structural (capturing relationships)
  - Behavioral (capturing dynamics)
- **Types**:
  - Visual representations (diagrams, drawings)
  - Mathematical representations (equations, formulas)
  - Linguistic representations (natural or formal language)
  - Computational representations (data structures)
- **Role**: Bridge between reality and understanding in all modeling activities

## Context
Representation is fundamental to all knowledge work. Effective representation balances fidelity with simplicity, enabling useful analysis while remaining comprehensible.

**Related terms**: model, abstraction, symbol
