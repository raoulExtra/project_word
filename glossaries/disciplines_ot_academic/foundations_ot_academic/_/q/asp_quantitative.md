# Quantitative

## Definition
Quantitative refers to the quality or state of being expressed as a number or measurable quantity, enabling mathematical manipulation and statistical analysis.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Provide numerical representation for analysis and computation
- **Key aspects**:
  - **Numerical expression**: Represented by numbers
  - **Measurability**: Can be quantified using standard units
  - **Comparability**: Values can be ordered and compared
- **Types**:
  - **Discrete**: Countable integers
  - **Continuous**: Measurable within ranges
  - **Dimensional**: Associated with physical units
- **Role**: Enables precise analysis, modeling, and computational reasoning

## Context
In core concepts, quantitative aspects are fundamental to scientific measurement, statistical analysis, and computational modeling. They distinguish numerical data from qualitative descriptions.

**Related terms**: measurement, attribute, property, numerical
