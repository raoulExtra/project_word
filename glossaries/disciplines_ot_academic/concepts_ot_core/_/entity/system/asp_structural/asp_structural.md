# Structural

## Definition
Structural aspect defines the physical, logical, or conceptual composition of a system - its parts, elements, components, and their relationships and connections.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Describe what a system is made of and how its parts are arranged
- **Key aspects**:
  - System elements (components, entities, parts)
  - Structural relationships (connections, interfaces, dependencies)
  - Hierarchical composition (subsystems, aggregation)
  - Physical or conceptual boundaries
- **Types**:
  - Physical structure (tangible components)
  - Logical structure (abstract relationships)
  - Conceptual structure (mental models)
- **Role**: Provides the foundation for understanding system composition and enabling structural analysis

## Context
Structural analysis distinguishes "what is" from "what does," forming the basis for both functional and behavioral understanding.
