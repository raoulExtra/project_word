# Behaviour

## Definition
Observable actions, responses, or patterns exhibited by a system in response to inputs or internal conditions.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Describe how a system responds to stimuli or operates
- **Key aspects**: Observable, measurable, predictable patterns
- **Types**: Internal behaviour, external behaviour, emergent behaviour
- **Role**: Enables understanding and prediction of system responses
