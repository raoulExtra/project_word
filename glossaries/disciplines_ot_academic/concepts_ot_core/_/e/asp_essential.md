# Essential

## Definition
Essential refers to something that is absolutely necessary, fundamental, or indispensable for the existence, function, or meaning of something else.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Identify what is indispensable vs. incidental
- **Characteristics**:
  - Necessary for existence or function
  - Cannot be removed without loss of identity
  - Fundamental rather than derivative
- **Types**:
  - Essential properties (define what something is)
  - Essential elements (required for function)
  - Essential characteristics (cannot be omitted)
- **Distinction**: Opposed to accidental or non-essential features

## Context
In philosophy and conceptual analysis, identifying what is essential is crucial for understanding the nature and identity of concepts, objects, and systems.
