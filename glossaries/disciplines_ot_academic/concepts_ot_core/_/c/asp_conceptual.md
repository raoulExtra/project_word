# Conceptual

## Definition
Conceptual refers to that which relates to concepts, ideas, or mental constructs, representing the fundamental units of thought and knowledge organization in cognitive and philosophical systems.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Identify and analyze mental representations and their structures
- **Key aspects**:
  - Conceptual structure (organization of mental representations)
  - Conceptual content (what concepts represent)
  - Conceptual change (how concepts evolve)
- **Types**:
  - Elementary concepts (basic, unanalyzable)
  - Complex concepts (composed of simpler concepts)
  - Prototype concepts (based on typical examples)
  - Exemplar concepts (based on stored instances)
- **Role**: Provides the framework for understanding how knowledge is organized and processed in minds

## Context
In core concepts, conceptual analysis is fundamental to cognitive science, linguistics, and philosophy of mind. It addresses how humans form, organize, and use mental representations to understand and interact with the world.
