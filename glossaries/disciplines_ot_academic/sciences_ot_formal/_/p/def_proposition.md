# Proposition

## Definition
A proposition is a declarative statement that expresses a mathematical or logical assertion that can be either true or false, serving as the fundamental unit of logical discourse.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Express mathematical truths and logical assertions
- **Characteristics**:
  - Declarative and truth-valued
  - Independent of linguistic structure
  - Can be atomic or compound
- **Types**:
  - Theorems (proven propositions)
  - Conjectures (unproven but suspected propositions)
  - Axioms (self-evident propositions)
- **Role**: Building blocks for mathematical theories and logical systems

## Context
In formal sciences, propositions are the primary objects of mathematical proof and logical analysis, forming the foundation for rigorous reasoning.
