# Physics

## Definition
Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its fundamental constituents, and the forces and resulting motions and changes of matter.

## Details
- **Focus**: Matter, energy, space-time, fundamental forces
- **Key concepts**: Motion, force, energy, mass, charge, waves
- **Branches**: Classical mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, relativity, quantum mechanics
- **Method**: Experimental verification, mathematical modeling

## Context
Physics is the foundational science that seeks to understand how the universe behaves at all scales, from subatomic particles to cosmic structures.
