# Mathematics

## Overview
Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, structures, space, and change through logical reasoning and proof.

## Major Areas
- **Number Theory** - integers and their properties
- **Algebra** - symbols and the rules for manipulating them
- **Geometry** - shapes, sizes, and spatial properties
- **Calculus/Analysis** - continuous change and limits
- **Discrete Mathematics** - countable structures
- **Logic** - formal reasoning and proof theory
- **Probability/Statistics** - uncertainty and data
