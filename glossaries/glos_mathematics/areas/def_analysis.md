# Analysis

## Definition
Analysis is the branch of mathematics concerned with limits, continuity, differentiation, integration, measure, infinite series, and their applications.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Rigorous study of continuity and change
- **Key concepts**: Limits, derivatives, integrals, sequences, series
- **Foundation**: Built on set theory and logic
- **Applications**: Mathematical physics, probability theory, economics

## Context
Analysis provides the theoretical foundations for calculus and extends mathematical rigor to infinite processes and abstract spaces.
