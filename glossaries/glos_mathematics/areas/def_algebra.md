# Algebra

## Definition
Algebra is the study of operations and the structures they form, using symbols to represent numbers and quantities in mathematical expressions.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Study of mathematical symbols and rules for manipulating them
- **Key concepts**: Variables, equations, polynomials, groups, rings, fields
- **Development**: Modern notation emerged during Renaissance
- **Branches**: Elementary algebra, abstract algebra, linear algebra

## Context
Algebra provides a unifying thread through almost all of mathematics and is foundational for advanced mathematical study.
