# Mathematics

## Definition
Mathematics is a field of knowledge concerned with abstract concepts such as numbers, geometric shapes, sets, functions, and probabilities. It uses logical reasoning and proof to study and establish their properties.

## Details
- **Purpose**: Study of abstract structures, quantities, and relations
- **Methods**: Logical reasoning, proof, axiomatic systems
- **Applications**: Science, engineering, technology, economics, everyday life
- **Independence**: Fundamental truths are independent of scientific experimentation

## Context
Mathematics is essential across all quantitative disciplines and provides the foundation for modeling and solving problems in natural sciences, engineering, medicine, finance, computer science, and social sciences.
